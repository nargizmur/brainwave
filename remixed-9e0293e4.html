About Us

WHO WE ARE

We are the Astana chapter of the International Youth Neuroscience Association (IYNA) — a global, youth-led nonprofit dedicated to inspiring the next generation of neuroscientists.

Our mission is to introduce concepts of neuroscience, mental health, and psychology to young people in Astana, raise awareness of neurological conditions, and connect our community with young neuroscientists worldwide. We foster scientific curiosity, critical thinking, and early engagement with life sciences and biotechnology.

Brain Wave Ideathon 2026 is the first neuroscience ideathon in Kazakhstan — a structured ideation competition where students develop real-world solutions grounded in neuroscience principles and pitch them to a professional jury.

The Competition

THE IDEATHON

The ideathon is a two-stage competition focused on creativity, scientific reasoning, and practical innovation — not memorization. Participants will identify a real-world problem relevant to neuroscience or human wellbeing, develop a solution grounded in neuroscience principles, refine their idea through coaching and peer feedback, and deliver a structured pitch to a professional jury panel.

Why It Matters

OUR GOALS

Kazakhstan is experiencing rapid growth in biotechnology, genomics, and life sciences. However, students often lack early exposure to real research environments and applied scientific fields such as genomics, molecular biology, and biomedical innovation.

Introduce students to applied thinking in neuroscience, biology, and biotech
02

03

04
